Business & Economy

STARTUPS

Tech Startups See Record Funding Boom

Venture capital investments in Indian AI-driven startups have doubled in the last quarter, reaching a record high of $2 Billion. Analysts suggest that the global interest in generative AI is fueling this surge. Bangalore continues to lead the pack, accounting for 60% of total deal volume.

However, experts warn of a potential bubble. "Valuations are skyrocketing without clear revenue models," cautioned a leading economist. Despite this, hiring in the sector has increased by 15% month-on-month.

CORPORATE

New Work-From-Home Guidelines

Major IT giants including TCS and Infosys are revising their remote work policies for the fiscal year 2026. The new "Hybrid 3.0" model mandates employees to be in the office for at least three days a week, aiming to foster better team collaboration.

Employee unions have pushed back, citing commute times and productivity loss. A recent survey showed that 70% of developers prefer full remote work. Management, however, insists that face-to-face interaction is crucial for innovation.
